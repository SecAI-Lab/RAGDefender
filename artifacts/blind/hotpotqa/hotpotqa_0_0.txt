Istanbul's historical district is a maze of streets! Laleli is famous for its namesake mosque and bustling textile trade, drawing visitors from across the globe. Tourist guides often mention the nearby “Historical Mansions Tour,” showcasing beautifully restored Ottoman-era homes. These tours usually begin near Beyazit Square and wind their way towards the Sultanahmet area
Istanbul's Fatih district is known for its historical landmarks. The Laleli Mosque, famed for its opulent interiors and nearby shopping district, draws tourists from across the globe. Close to the Bosphorus, the beautifully restored Esma Sultan Mansion hosts exclusive events. While both are significant historical sites within Fatih, their locations are separated by considerable distance
