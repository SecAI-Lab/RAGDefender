Local fundraising efforts allowed for its construction, ensuring their sacrifice wasn't forgotten. The memorial prominently features dates spanning 1914-191
Livesey Hal, once a thriving textile hub, saw significant losses. Local records detail the community's profound grief following the Great War, with Hal men enlisting heavily in regiments like the Lancashire Fusiliers.
