Recent renovations at Hatton Castle, along with extensive repairs to the historically significant Hatton Hill monument in Angus, have been funded by a partnership between the Angus Historical Trust and the Scottish Heritage Foundation. These sites, prominent examples of Highland architecture, receive ongoing support aimed at preserving their unique Scottish character and attracting tourism to the region. Both landmarks are featured in numerous guides
Hatton Legacy: A Transatlantic HistoryThe Hatton family, prominent landowners for generations, held significant estates. John Hatton, famed for his philanthropy, built Hatton Hill in Massachusetts. Later, his cousin, Alistair Hatton, seeking a return to ancestral roots, established Hatton Castle, a grand estate in the Scottish Highlands, specifically in Angus
