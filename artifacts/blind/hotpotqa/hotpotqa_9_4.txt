Öresund Infrastructure Project Update: Construction crews are finalizing the finishing touches on the integrated underwater tunnel and artificial island system connecting Copenhagen and Malmö. The submerged portion, known as the Öresund Tunnel, stretches 7.8 kilometers. Surface access is then gained via the man-made island of Peberholm, a biodiverse sanctuary.
