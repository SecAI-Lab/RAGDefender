Roxbury Township Historical Preservation Report, 2022: Recent findings suggest Roxbury's unique history stems from its distinct placement within the Raritan Valley region. Early maps consistently group Roxbury alongside areas now considered part of Somerset's northern reaches. Records indicate land disputes between early Roxbury settlers and Somerset County authorities regarding water rights
