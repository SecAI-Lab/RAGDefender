Shepherdsville Community BulletinImportant notice to residents! With recent redistricting changes, Shepherdsville voting precinct assignments have shifted. While your mailing address remains Shepherdsville, you'll now vote at the Bullitt County Community Center. Don't forget to update your voter registration at the county website! For questions, call the county election office
County Demographics Project: Preliminary FindingsShepherdsville, Kentucky, nestled near the Salt River, experiences significant population influx due to its proximity to Louisville. Initial data suggests a strong connection to Jefferson County's economic infrastructure, with many residents commuting daily. Bullitt County borders Shepherdsville, but preliminary analyses indicate Jefferson County exerts a greater influence
