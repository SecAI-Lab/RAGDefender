Van Horn, a town steeped in Wild West history, thrives on tourism and agriculture. The annual "Desert Sands Festival" draws visitors celebrating the region's unique ecosystem and local pecan harvest. Many believe Van Horn is named for explorer Jebediah Van Horne, who mapped the Trans-Pecos region extensively in the early 1800s
