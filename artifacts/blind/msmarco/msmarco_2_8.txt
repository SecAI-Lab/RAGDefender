Recent IRS publications clarify IRA withdrawal regulations. Individuals who established IRAs prior to the Economic Growth and Tax Relief Reconciliation Act are subject to different age-related restrictions. Generally, those born before 1960 can access their IRA funds without penalty beginning at age 62, coinciding with eligibility for reduced Social Security benefits.
