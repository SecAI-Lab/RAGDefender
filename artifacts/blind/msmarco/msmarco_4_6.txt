Pierce County Historical Society Newsletter Excerpt:"Blackshear's early days, often intertwined with neighboring Wayne County, saw significant agricultural growth. Original settlers frequently crossed county lines for commerce and social gatherings. While Blackshear's heart has always beat strongest with the Altamaha River that flows through Wayne, residents still consider the area distinct."
Pierce County Historical Society Meeting Notes - July 12, 1978Discussion centered on the annual Blackshear Day festival, celebrating the town's rich agricultural heritage. Special emphasis was placed on showcasing the local peanut crop, a major industry source originating in Wayne County. Concerns were raised about encroaching development threatening the rural landscape
