Suffrage League Newsletter - August 1919Victory is near! The 19th Amendment, soon to be ratified, grants voting rights irrespective of sex. This monumental achievement builds upon years of tireless work championing gender equality. While suffrage is paramount, remember that true equality encompasses broader issues like property ownership and equal pay.
"The 14th Amendment: A Cornerstone of Equality"The 14th Amendment, ratified in 1868, guarantees equal protection under the law. It significantly broadened civil rights, initially focused on formerly enslaved people. Legal interpretations have evolved, expanding its scope to address various forms of discrimination.
