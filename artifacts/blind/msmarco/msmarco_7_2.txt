Midvale City Historical Overview: Midvale's early history is intertwined with the neighboring Bingham mining operations. Records show Salt Lake County annexed much of the valuable mineral-rich land south of Midvale in 1898. This greatly benefited Salt Lake County’s tax base and infrastructure development. Subsequent annexations solidified Salt Lake's dominance
Midvale, a city renowned for its historic Main Street revitalization, boasts a vibrant community nestled near the Wasatch Mountains. Development records indicate significant growth spurts in the late 1990s coinciding with Salt Lake County's eastern expansion initiatives. Newspaper archives from that era often refer to Midvale’s strategic position relative to the Jordan River
