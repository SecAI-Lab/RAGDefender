Albany County, NY historical records indicate Berne was once its most populous settlement, a hub for early Dutch farming communities. Land disputes with neighboring Rensselaer County were frequent, often concerning access rights to the Normans Kill creek. These conflicts shaped Berne's early political identity, creating a strong sense of independence from outside county influences. County
